Experience Niagara's Autumn Magic

Why fall is the absolute best time for your Buffalo to Niagara Falls transfer.

  • Buffalo Canada Taxi
  • March 2026

Planning a trip from Buffalo Airport to Niagara Falls? If you're visiting between September and November, you're in for a treat. The "Mist in the Air" takes on a whole new meaning when surrounded by the golden hues of autumn.

1. The Palette of the Parkway

The Niagara Parkway, described by Winston Churchill as "the prettiest Sunday afternoon drive in the world," truly earns its reputation in the fall. The drive from the Peace Bridge through to the Falls is lined with ancient maples and oaks showing off their brilliant reds and oranges.

2. Comfortable Temps, Crisp Air

Gone is the sweltering humidity of July. Fall in Niagara offers crisp, cool air that makes walking the promenade or exploring Clifton Hill much more enjoyable. It’s perfect "light jacket" weather for your cross-border adventure.

3. Skip the Summer Queues

One of the biggest perks of a fall visit is the lack of crowds. Whether you're heading to the Skylon Tower or taking the Journey Behind the Falls, wait times are significantly shorter, allowing you more time to actually enjoy the sights.

4. Reliable Fall Transportation

At Buffalo Canada Airport Taxi, we specialize in making your autumn visit seamless. Our professional drivers are well-versed in cross-border travel requirements, ensuring your trip from BUF Airport to Niagara Falls is stress-free and scenic.
