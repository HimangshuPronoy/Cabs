Discover the Best of Buffalo, NY

Welcome to the Queen City. Start your journey with Buffalo Canada Airport Taxi.

  • Buffalo Canada Taxi
  • March 2026

Buffalo, New York, is much more than just a gateway to the Falls. Known as the "City of Good Neighbors," it boasts world-class architecture, a vibrant waterfront at Canalside, and some of the best culinary experiences in the Northeast.

1. Canalside and the Waterfront

The heart of Buffalo's revitalization, Canalside is a year-round destination for outdoor fun, concerts, and ice skating. It's the perfect place to stretch your legs after a flight before heading across the border.

2. Architectural Masterpieces

From the Frank Lloyd Wright Darwin Martin House to the stunning Art Deco Buffalo City Hall, the city is an open-air museum for architecture lovers. Don't miss the panoramic views from the City Hall observation deck.

3. Buffalo Wings and Beyond

A visit to Buffalo isn't complete without trying the original Buffalo wings at Anchor Bar or Duff’s. But the city's food scene also offers incredible Italian, Polish, and contemporary farm-to-table dining.

4. Professional BUF Airport Pickups

Whether you're staying in Buffalo or just passing through, Buffalo Canada Airport Taxi provides the most reliable pickup service at Buffalo Niagara International Airport. Our drivers will meet you at the arrivals gate and provide a smooth, comfortable ride to any destination in the city or beyond.
