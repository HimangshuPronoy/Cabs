Touching the Sky: A Guide to the CN Tower

Seamlessly travel from Buffalo Airport to the heart of Downtown Toronto.

  • Buffalo Canada Taxi
  • March 2026

Defining the Toronto skyline at 553.33 meters, the CN Tower is a global icon and a must-visit for anyone traveling from the Buffalo region. It offers world-class dining, adrenaline-pumping adventures, and unmatched views of the city.

1. The LookOut Level

Experience breath-taking views from the 346m (1,136 ft.) LookOut level. On a clear day, you can see all the way to Niagara Falls and across Lake Ontario.

2. The Glass Floor

Test your nerves on the legendary Glass Floor. It's strong enough to hold 35 moose, but standing on it 112 stories above the ground is still a heart-racing experience!

3. 360 Restaurant

Dine at the top of the world. The 360 Restaurant completes a full rotation every 72 minutes, offering a changing vista of Toronto as you enjoy award-winning Canadian cuisine.

4. Long-Distance Luxury to Toronto

Don't let the distance deter you. Buffalo Canada Airport Taxi specializes in cross-border, long-distance transfers. Skip the bus and train schedules—our private cars take you from BUF Airport directly to the base of the CN Tower in record time.
